Our Services

We provide structured academic placements and observational hours across our core child therapy and special education service tracks.

Speech Therapy

Observe clinical evaluation of speech delays, articulation guides, phonetic training, and communication tools designed for non-verbal children.

Occupational Therapy

Gain exposure to sensory integration therapies, fine motor skill exercises, balance enhancement, and sensory-friendly environmental adaptations.

Behaviour Modification Program

Shadow psychologists applying Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA), token economy systems, and positive reinforcement to handle behavioral challenges.

Child Physiotherapy

Learn Gross Motor Function Classification (GMFCS) tracking, gross motor exercises, posture corrections, and parallel-bar gait rehabilitation.

Special Education

Shadow certified special educators as they compile Individualized Education Plans (IEPs), teach basic arithmetic, and design interactive learning boards.

Vocational Training

Participate in workshops training teenagers in candle decorations, paper cup and plate manufacturing, packaging, and micro-business operation basics.

ADL Training

Observe self-care training systems for Activities of Daily Living, instructing children in self-feeding, dressing, basic hygiene, and grooming.

Aqua Therapy

Understand specialized water-based muscular coordination exercises, vestibular balance therapies, and physical sensory relaxation techniques.

Child, Adult & Couple Counselling

Shadow counseling sessions for children, parental support counseling, marital counseling support, and community mental health campaigns.

Internship Curriculum

3-Month Roadmap

A structured, milestone-based timeline designed to give interns comprehensive hands-on exposure to clinical diagnostics and special needs rehabilitation.

Month 1

Orientation, RPWD Act Studies & Observation

Get acclimated with the clinical environment and understand the core operational frameworks of the day school:

  • Study the 21 disability categories mapped under the RPWD Act, 2016.
  • Analyze historical patient files to track clinical history and progress.
  • Observe clinical setups and understand sensory-friendly layouts.
Month 2

IEP Designs & Diagnostic Shadowing

Step into drafting active interventions and assisting clinical experts with daily evaluations:

  • Assist senior educators in drafting Individualized Education Plans (IEPs).
  • Log daily cognitive and motor behavior profiling indicators.
  • Observe audiometric assessments, speech therapy planning, and sensory diagnostics.
Month 3

Active Clinical Shadowing & Case Study Reports

Complete casework logs and submit clinical evidence reports for university review:

  • Shadow occupational, behavior modification, and physiotherapy sessions closely.
  • Complete detailed case histories of two children based on observation notes.
  • Receive placement certification and performance evaluation reports from the trust.
