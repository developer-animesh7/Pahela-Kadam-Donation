Special Education

Assist special educators in creating and monitoring Individualized Education Plans (IEPs) for our students.

3-Month Internship Curriculum

A structured, milestone-based timeline designed to give interns comprehensive hands-on exposure to clinical diagnostics and special needs rehabilitation.

Month 1: Orientation & Observational Shadowing

Understand the 21 disability categories under the RPWD Act. Read historic patient files, observe therapists, and understand sensory-friendly classroom layouts.

Month 2: IEP Design & Diagnostic Shadowing

Assist senior clinicians in preparing Individualized Education Plans (IEPs). Log daily cognitive profiling and help draft therapy milestones.

Month 3: Clinical Practice & Case Study Reporting

Shadow speech and occupational therapy sessions. Complete and submit a detailed casework profile of two students as part of university submission guidelines.
