Pahela Kadam supports children with autism, intellectual disabilities, cerebral palsy, down syndrome, and developmental delays. Admissions are entirely free of charge for children from underprivileged families, following an initial pediatric and special educational diagnostic evaluation.

Corporate partners can sponsor specialized class digitizations, child pediatric therapy standing frames, or fund vocational cafe placement tracks. The trust is registered on the MCA portal (CSR Registration No: CSR00039230) for tax-exempt CSR partnerships.
