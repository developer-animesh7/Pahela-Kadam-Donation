🦽 Assistive Devices
Verified Campaign 8 Children Benefited

Pediatric Mobility & Walkers

For children with cerebral palsy and severe motor disorders, taking a single step is a major milestone. Pahela Kadam provides custom-made supportive walkers, ankle-foot orthoses, and gait-training tools. With daily guidance from our physical therapists, these children build muscle memory, improve their balance, and discover the joy of movement. Your support directly funds custom adaptive mobility devices and weekly physical rehabilitation sessions for children waiting to stand on their own feet.
