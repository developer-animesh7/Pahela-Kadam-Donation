ABOUT US

Helping Today,
Building Tomorrow.

Welcome to Pahela Kadam. Established in Dhanbad, Jharkhand, and operated by the Narayani Charitable Trust, we are a dedicated day school and rehabilitation center. Our core mission is to empower children with intellectual disabilities, autism, cerebral palsy, visual, hearing, and multiple physical challenges. Through a structured combination of special education, advanced therapies, and vocational training, we help these special children break through developmental limits to lead self-reliant lives.

Read Our Story

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower children with intellectual, sensory, and physical challenges by providing comprehensive therapeutic care, specialized education, and adaptive vocational training. By creating targeted developmental milestones, we aim to integrate these special children into mainstream society, restoring their personal dignity, securing their human rights, and building the necessary self-reliance for them to thrive in their daily lives.

Our Vision

Our vision is a society where children with developmental and physical disabilities are fully valued, respected, and given equal opportunities to live with pride. We envision a future where specialized pediatric therapies and inclusive developmental systems are accessible to every special child, regardless of socioeconomic background, enabling them to achieve independence and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

Mrs. Anita Agarwal

Founder and Inspirational Director, Pahela Kadam
OUR JOURNEY

How We Began & Where We Stand

Founder's Inspiration

Recognizing the acute lack of therapeutic care and cognitive integration for children with developmental limits in Dhanbad, Mrs. Anita Agarwal took the initiative to set up a comprehensive support center.

Humble Beginnings (2008)

Pahela Kadam launched its operational journey in a modest layout in 2008, initially providing caring education and basic therapy to just two special needs children.

Growing Community & Family Trust

As children began exhibiting measurable milestones in articulation, movement, and cognitive behavior, local families increasingly placed their trust in our school, expanding student registration.

NGO & Corporate Collaborations

Collaboration with various national NGOs, social enterprises, and corporates helped fund high-end tools, interactive sensory integration labs, and specialized clinical rooms.

Today Supporting 150+ Children

Today, Pahela Kadam operates as a premier day school and comprehensive therapy lab serving over 150 special needs children with direct daily physical, vocational, and social integration support.

⭐

National Recognition

Mrs. Anita Agarwal's lifelong dedication to empowering children with disabilities has earned recognition from national leaders. Her work reflects years of compassion, inclusive education, and unwavering commitment to building a better future for every child.
