From Silence to Expression: Khushi's Path

When Khushi joined Pahela Kadam School at age 6, she struggled with non-verbal speech barriers and high sensory sensitivity, making it difficult to interact with her family.

Through continuous speech therapy sessions and customized sensory integration tools, Khushi began utilizing verbal word pairs. Today, she expresses her needs and smiles confidently in class.
